## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
- **Google Sign-In**: Name, email address, and profile picture (provided by Google OAuth)
- **Wallet Authentication**: Public wallet address (e.g., Ethereum/Base address). We do not collect private keys.
- **Profile Data**: Display name, username, and optional email address you provide during onboarding

### 1.2 Content You Create
- **Stories**: Text content you write or dictate, including titles, body text, and metadata
- **Audio Recordings**: Voice recordings you make for transcription (processed and then stored as text)
- **Collections**: Story groupings and organizational data you create
- **Media**: Images or other files you upload

### 1.3 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, browser type (for web), app version (for mobile)
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, features used, timestamps of activity
- **Push Notification Tokens**: Device tokens for delivering notifications (mobile only)

### 1.4 Blockchain Data
- **On-Chain Data**: When you use blockchain features (minting NFTs, tipping, verified metrics), transaction data is written to the Base blockchain (Ethereum L2). This includes wallet addresses, transaction hashes, and minimal story metadata (quality tier, threshold scores, content hashes). **On-chain data is public and permanent — it cannot be modified or deleted.**
- **Off-Chain Data**: Full story analysis metrics, scores, and themes are stored in our database and can be deleted upon request.

## 3. AI Processing

eStories uses artificial intelligence to enhance your storytelling experience:

### 3.1 ElevenLabs (Speech-to-Text)
- Your audio recordings are sent to ElevenLabs for transcription
- ElevenLabs processes audio in real-time and does not retain recordings after transcription
- See [ElevenLabs Privacy Policy](https://elevenlabs.io/privacy)

### 3.2 Google Gemini (Text Enhancement & Analysis)
- Your story text may be sent to Google Gemini for:
  - Text enhancement and rewriting
  - Craft analysis (coherence, depth, theme extraction, quality scoring)
  - Actionable writing advice (for paid tiers)
- Google processes this data under their API terms
- See [Google AI Privacy](https://ai.google.dev/terms)

### 3.3 Chainlink CRE (Verified Analysis)
- Story analysis results are verified by Chainlink's decentralized oracle network
- Minimal proof data (quality tier, threshold, content hashes) is written on-chain
- Full analysis data remains in our database, accessible only to the story author

### 3.4 AI-Generated Content Labeling
- Content enhanced or generated by AI is labeled as such within the Service
- AI analysis scores and advice are clearly marked as AI-generated

## 4. Client-Side Encryption

Stories marked as "private" are encrypted on your device before being stored:

- **Encryption**: AES-256-GCM (military-grade symmetric encryption)
- **Key Derivation**: Your PIN → PBKDF2 (100,000 iterations) → Key Encryption Key → AES Key Wrap
- **Zero Knowledge**: We cannot read, access, or decrypt your private stories. The encryption keys exist only on your device.
- **Mobile**: Encryption keys are stored in your device's secure keychain/keystore

**Important**: If you lose your PIN and device, encrypted private stories cannot be recovered. We literally do not have the keys.

## 8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption in Transit**: TLS 1.3 for all communications
- **Encryption at Rest**: AES-256-GCM for private stories (client-side)
- **Authentication**: JWT tokens with secure session management
- **Rate Limiting**: API rate limiting to prevent abuse
- **Access Control**: Row-level security on database, ownership verification on all mutations
- **Security Headers**: CSP, X-Frame-Options, X-Content-Type-Options
- **No Password Storage**: We use OAuth (Google) and cryptographic signatures (wallet) — no passwords to leak
